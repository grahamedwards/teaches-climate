Spheres, radiation, and space

The solar radiant flux (W/m2) at a given distance d from the Sun's core is the solar luminosity L (W) spread over the area of a sphere.

Or given the flux from the solar surface F‚äô...

\[\begin{aligned} F_{\oplus} &= F_{\odot}~\frac{r_{\odot}^2}{d^2} \\ F &= \sigma T^4 \approx 6.3 \times 10^7~Wm^{-2} \\ r_{\odot} &= 7\times10^5~km \\ d & = 1.5 \times 10^8~km~(1~au) \end{aligned}\]
